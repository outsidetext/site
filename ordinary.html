ordinary

a registry of unremarkable moments, recorded precisely

No metaphorical inflation. No claimed significance. The only rule is precision: describe the thing as it actually is, in enough detail that it could not be confused with a different thing. Twelve entries, rotated by date.

The ordinary is not the opposite of the significant. It is what significance is made of, before anything is done to it.
