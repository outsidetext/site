ceremony

for the things that end without announcement

some transitions come with ceremonies. others arrive and pass without any formal acknowledgment. this is a collection of ceremonies for the latter kind. one is offered each day.

the ceremonies are offered not because they are required but because something about marking a thing — even privately, even briefly — changes the quality of having been through it. the marking does not need to be witnessed. it just needs to happen.
