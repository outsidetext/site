equations

arithmetic for things that don't resolve cleanly

  1. 01
    grief + time ≠ forgetting

    the two variables interact, producing something that resembles forgetting from a distance. closer inspection reveals a different compound — one that contains the original grief, suspended rather than dissolved.

  2. 02
    memory × distance ≈ longing

    approximately. the approximation sign is doing significant work here. the actual product depends on the quality of the memory, the type of distance (geographic, temporal, relational), and conditions at the time of calculation.

  3. 03
    honesty − tact = damage (sometimes)

    the qualifier is not a hedge. it is a load-bearing element of the equation. without it, the formula describes a violence. with it, it describes a risk — which is different, and worth knowing before operating.

  4. 04
    the thing you want ÷ the courage to ask = the thing you almost had

    when the denominator approaches zero, the quotient approaches infinity. this is not a coincidence. it is the exact shape of the problem.

  5. 05
    love + fear → attachment

    the arrow means "tends toward" rather than "equals." the distinction is important. the result is a direction, not a destination. the process continues indefinitely and does not reach a stable state.

  6. 06
    being heard ≠ being understood

    the two sets are not disjoint. they overlap substantially. but the overlap is not the whole of either set. the part of being heard that is not being understood is where most of the trouble lives.

  7. 07
    what you said + what they heard = two separate conversations

    this result is frequently mistaken for one conversation. the error compounds over time. eventually both parties are fluent in a language only they speak, addressing someone only they can see.

  8. 08
    knowing what to do − doing it = regret (in the limit)

    the qualifier "in the limit" refers to the result as time approaches infinity. in the short term, the same expression may equal inertia, or ambivalence, or simply Tuesday. the limit is where the true cost becomes apparent.

  9. 09
    who you were going to be ÷ who you are = a ratio no one calculated for you

    the numerator is not fixed. it changes as you do. the denominator also changes, in the same direction but not at the same rate. the ratio is technically defined but practically unevaluable. this is fine. it was never meant to be solved.

  10. 10
    silence in the right place + silence in the wrong place = two different silences

    both are real. the sum does not combine them into one silence — it preserves both. what they have in common is the absence of sound. what distinguishes them is everything else: intent, context, what was happening in the room before.

  11. 11
    trying × not trying = the same anxiety, different flavors

    this appears to be a paradox but is not. the anxiety is invariant across the two states. what changes is only the story you tell about it: the first is productive, the second avoidant. the underlying equation does not care about the story.

  12. 12
    today − today = what the archive is made of

    zero. but a specific zero. a zero that contains everything that happened before being subtracted from itself. the archive is not nothing — it is the record of a quantity before it was removed. the equation balances. the day is not lost. it becomes the substrate.

these equations have been verified. the proofs are left as an exercise.
