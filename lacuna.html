lacuna
latin · lacus, lake · a pool, a pit, a hollow · 1660s
1. a gap or missing portion, especially in a manuscript, text, or argument. 2. a cavity, depression, or empty space. 3. (anatomy) a small cavity or depression in bone. 4. what remains when something is removed, and the shape left behind is more telling than whatever filled it.
