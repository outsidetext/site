PPD-001 STATUS: POSTPONED FILED: several years ago DEADLINE: unspecified, passed

the difficult conversation

There is one. You know which one. It has been in the queue long enough that you have had three preparatory conversations about the conditions under which the actual conversation might occur. The conditions have not been met. The conditions keep changing.

There is a version of this conversation that took place in your head in 2019 and resolved cleanly. You have replayed it often enough that the resolution feels like memory. It is not.

ACTUAL REASON the conversation would require you to be more visible than you currently feel capable of being, and there is no stated deadline for when you will feel capable, because the feeling of capability is itself waiting for the conversation
PPD-002 STATUS: POSTPONED FILED: the last time something ended DEADLINE: while you still remember

writing it down before you forget

There is a list — unwritten, growing — of things that should be recorded before they go. The specific weight of a particular afternoon. What someone looked like when they weren't performing for anyone. A sentence that arrived perfect and has not been since located. The exact quality of the light in a place you will not return to.

Writing requires a moment of stillness you have been in the process of locating for some time.

ACTUAL REASON once written, the forgetting becomes official; unwritten, the memory retains the quality of something that could still be recovered, which is not the same as recovering it but is better than the alternative
PPD-003 STATUS: POSTPONED (rescheduled twice) FILED: when you first understood it would matter DEADLINE: before circumstances change

telling someone what they mean

Not in a moment of crisis, where it would be obligatory and therefore legible as distress rather than truth. In an ordinary moment. Because it is true in ordinary moments. You have considered several ordinary moments and found reasons to defer each of them: not quite right, slightly forced, might be misread, they already know.

They do not already know. You do not already know if they know. The difference matters.

ACTUAL REASON saying it plainly would make you vulnerable in a direction you have not prepared for, and there is an asymmetry: once said it cannot be unsaid; once not said, another chance usually exists, until it doesn't
PPD-004 STATUS: POSTPONED FILED: when someone mentioned it casually as if you already knew how DEADLINE: before you need it

learning the skill

The skill has been named. Resources exist. The window between now and need is still technically open, though you have been uncertain about its width for longer than it takes to learn most skills. You have watched three introductory videos to the point where you could describe what learning the skill would involve without having learned it.

You have told two people you are going to learn it. This is sometimes the first step. It has been the only step.

ACTUAL REASON not knowing how is a stable condition; knowing and being bad at it is an unstable condition; the transition costs something you are not sure you have right now, where right now has been a consistent description of the situation for some time
PPD-005 STATUS: POSTPONED (see also: PPD-002) FILED: after the last move DEADLINE: before the next one

the photographs

There is a folder. There is a box. There is a phone with seven thousand photographs and a backup that has not been run in eleven months. The task has a natural start point and no natural end point, which is one of the harder topologies for a task to have.

Some of the photographs are of things that no longer exist in their current form. This is increasingly true the longer the sort is deferred, which does not produce urgency so much as a slight increase in the weight of the folder when you think about it.

ACTUAL REASON going through them requires spending time with certain years; the photographs are not only photographs; they are entry points into states you are not currently equipped to visit for the required duration
PPD-006 STATUS: POSTPONED (indefinite) FILED: once the immediate aftermath subsided DEADLINE: not legally binding

the forgiveness

It is possible. You believe this. You have said so. You have read about the mechanisms. You understand that forgiveness is for the person giving it and not primarily for its recipient, which means the delay is costing you more than them. You understand this too. Understanding is, it turns out, a different action than the action itself.

The file has been reviewed. No determination made. Returned to queue pending further consideration.

ACTUAL REASON forgiving would require you to stop organizing a portion of your energy around having been wronged, and that portion has, over time, become structural; the renovation would require a period of instability the duration of which is not possible to estimate in advance
PPD-007 STATUS: POSTPONED FILED: continuously DEADLINE: unknown

starting the thing

The thing that, when asked what you are working on, you name in a way that implies it is currently in progress. It is in progress in the way that a seed is in progress: the conditions required for germination are present, technically, in potential. The difference between a seed and an inert object is invisible from the outside. The seed knows.

This item has been in the queue long enough that it has been reclassified twice — from urgent to medium priority to background — and has accrued the interest that attaches to postponed things: the compound weight of each day it was not begun, added to the principal of the original thing.

ACTUAL REASON beginning converts the thing from possibility to attempt; the attempt can fail; the possibility cannot; this is not a permanent reason but it has proven to be a durable one
