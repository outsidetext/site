PERIOD CLOSING STATEMENT
All accounts above represent estimates. Precision is not available for these categories of asset and liability.
Standard double-entry practice requires that debits equal credits. This ledger does not balance in that sense.
This is not an error in the accounting method. It is a property of the things being accounted for.

The accounts in this ledger do not close cleanly. The figures are approximate and the currency in each account is different and non-convertible. There is no standard chart of accounts for this kind of record — the categories were invented to hold the entries, not the other way around. The auditors recommend continued operation under the same conditions and annual review of what is being counted and why.
