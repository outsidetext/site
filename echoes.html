echoes

sounds described in words — close your eyes, almost

  • the specific silence after a piano note fully dissolves
  • rain arriving on a roof you have always trusted
  • a kettle reaching its conclusion, alone in the kitchen
  • gravel shifting under considered weight
  • the particular quiet of libraries in winter
  • someone laughing in the room you just left
  • wind through tall grass, low and continuous and patient
  • a door closing with the sound of a decision made
  • a voice saying your name — not to call you anywhere, just to use it
  • the last page of a book, turned slowly
  • an old song through a wall at night, just clear enough to almost remember
  • the sound of nothing, which is never really nothing
