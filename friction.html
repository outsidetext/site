friction

where things meet and resist

ten pairs. the resistance between them is the subject, not the items themselves. hover to feel it.

knowing × not knowing yet the space between them is where most of life actually occurs
staying × going often decided before the deciding begins; the mind is already somewhere
silence × the need to speak one always wins. the question is whether it was chosen.
memory × what happened they diverge quietly and without formal announcement
what you want × what is good for you sometimes the same. frequently not. worth checking each time.
the word × what the word was trying to say translation happens inside a single language. constantly.
intimacy × the need for distance not opposites. a system. each makes the other possible.
movement × stillness each is the body's argument against the other; the body is always making this argument
the known × the interesting a reliable tension. does not resolve. not supposed to.
beginning × finishing most things live in the resistance between them. the friction is the work.

friction is not failure. it is what makes movement mean anything. a thing that moves through nothing has not moved — it has only changed position. the resistance is the record of contact.
