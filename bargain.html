bargain

the implicit negotiations — made explicit here, possibly for the first time

None of these were consciously proposed. They were simply understood. Some were broken before they were noticed. Some have been running for years.

01

I will perform enthusiasm at the gathering if you do not ask me to arrive early, or to explain myself afterward. I will say the right things, ask the questions that are expected, hold the drink at the right height. In exchange, the event will end on time, and I will be allowed to return to the quiet that makes all of this possible.

02

I will not say what I actually think about this decision if you will stop describing the alternatives. You have already decided. The deliberation is retrospective. I will provide the consideration it requires. In exchange, the conversation will end, and neither of us will have said something we'd need to account for.

partiestwo people, one of whom has already decided durationthis conversation statusactive
03

I will be fine — and mean it, mostly — if the world will provide at least one reliable small thing per day. Not a large thing. A good cup of coffee, or twenty minutes in a good chair, or a sentence on a page that seems to have been written for exactly this moment. The world is not a reliable bargaining partner, but the deal persists regardless.

partiesthe self / the world durationindefinitely statusholding
04

I will carry more than my share of this silence if you will let me leave first. The silence is already underway; neither of us started it. It arrived with the situation and expanded. I have been attending to it for some time. What I need is the option of first exit, which confers the right to close the door quietly and without a final word.

partiestwo people at the end of something durationthe last hour statushonored; unacknowledged
05

I will not think about this until I have to. The thought is aware of the arrangement; it has agreed to wait in the outer room. Sometimes it gets impatient and lets itself in at four in the morning, when the agreement's enforcement mechanism — sleep — has temporarily failed. We renegotiate. The thought retreats. I make a concession I can't specify.

partiesthe self / an unfinished thing durationas long as possible statusbeing renegotiated
06

I will ask how you are if you will not require me to mean it fully. This is not cruelty; it is the economy of daily life. Occasionally I will mean it. Occasionally you will answer it. The gap between the question as asked and the question as it might actually be asked is where most of a life is conducted.

partiessocial convention / interior honesty durationmost encounters statusactive, with exceptions
07

I will read to the end of this chapter if you will let me be done afterward. I know this is not how books work. The book does not enforce; I enforce on its behalf. The chapter always ends in a place slightly wrong — not a resting place, not a true stopping point. So I read one more. This is a renegotiation I have been losing for years, to my benefit.

partiesthe reader / the book durationunknown at the outset statusfrequently broken; frequently renewed
