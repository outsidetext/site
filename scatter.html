a chart of small moments — axes unlabeled

hover any point to read it

The axes are not labeled. They suggest something. You may be reading them differently than intended. Both readings may be correct.

one possible interpretation

x-axis: how long ago it happened. left is years, right is recent. y-axis: how often it still surfaces without being summoned. the size of each point is the weight it carried at the time. that weight and its current frequency do not always correspond. several old things float very high. several recent things rest near the bottom.
