    a note on notes

    A chord is three or more notes sounding at once. Most emotional vocabulary has a harmonic equivalent — a cluster of things that happen simultaneously and cannot be reduced to any single element. The word for a chord's emotional character is its affect. This page has one affect per month. Whether it matches the month is a matter of interpretation.
